Lightweight Solutions

Combined capabilities of DEITEL in product design, engineering, material science and manufacturing processes has helped develop many challenging solutions for the industry in weight reduction and replacement engineering. One of the key areas we support using our strengths is light weight engineering solutions for the emerging markets in automotive and aerospace industry. We support the industry in weight reduction programs using light weight composite solutions. Conversion of metallic parts to equivalent composites parts, multi-material (hybrid parts) are areas where we support customers in developing new products.
